What is Fabrication?

Metal fabrication can mean a lot of things to a lot of people. We define it as meaning changing an existing form of material into something else. This can mean welding, punching, bending, forming, any of a myriad of processes. Whatever it may mean to you we can help. In addition to the processes listed to the left we have extensive experience in tube fabrication.

We have the ability to provide high volume burr free tube cutting. This capability when combined with our mill relationships means we can often provide your larger volume cut tubing requirements for less than you could buy the tubing in length. In addition to straight cutting we can also...

  • Mitre Cut
  • Bend
  • Pierce
  • Centerless Grind
  • Chamfer
  • Hone
  • Swage
  • Bulge
  • Weld
  • Machine

Materials available:

  • Carbon Steel

  • Aluminum
  • Copper
